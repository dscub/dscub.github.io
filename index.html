Developer Student Clubs (DSC) is a program presented by Google Developers. By joining a DSC, students will develop their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. For the first time, Google Developers has identified UB to offer its Student Developer Club. DSC UB aims to impact students, and empower them to bring positive changes to our communities through technology. Together, we can build a better tomorrow.
